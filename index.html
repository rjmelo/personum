This is what I do

(professionally speaking)

I code

Software Engineering is just about writing lines of code, right? Well... gladly, it isn't, but code is the matter that cool stuff is made of. The feeling I get when I'm writing code reminds me of the freedom I felt and the creativity that I developed when I was just a child playing with my LEGO© bricks. And yes, I still play with LEGO© bricks. Oh yeah, you bet I do.

I manage

During my career I already had the opportunity to wear many hats. Some of them were managing hats. I've hopped between the techical and the commercial silos, with small-to-medium sized teams. It's a hard job to mediate all parties involved in a software project, but I love to play that role. Because code is cool, but ultimately it's always made for people.

I party

It's hard to see me in a bad mood (don't test me, though). I strive to create a positive atmosphere around me. Celebrating achievements and overcoming defeats with a naughty smile (we'll kick ass next time!) is my mantra. Don't expect me to be too formal: I can do that, but that's not me. At work, as everywhere, I always have a laugh promptly available and for free.
